Virtual Therapy

"Cognitive Rewiring through mixed-reality"

“Virtual Therapy is a 3D audio feedback system created in Unity to help those with visual impairment and lower-back disability. It focuses on improving and correcting the posture of users through various iterations of pleasant sounds. We hope this can be used globally, and positively affect those who require it”

Requirement Gathering

Approach 1 - Structural Interview

In order to obtain client requirements we had used the method of email communication to obtain more knowledge of the project.

Approach 2 - Online Questionnaire

An online Questionnaire was sent to the users physiatrist to retrieve specfic information in order to create the solution that was required to help him. The information was valuable as our project would circulate around these requirements.

Approach 3 - Skype Interview

After gaining an insight of what the project entails we finally arranged a suitable date to skype interview our client. Questions were thought of prior to the interview.

Why did we choose this?

  • Build a friendly and trustworthy relationship with the client
  • Clear any doubts about current mock-requirements
  • Clarify our budget, skills and what is do-able in the given time frame

Storyboard

MoSCoW Requirements

Must Have
  1. Gather 3D co-ordiantes from the environment and transform into an angle
  2. Consider the following parts of the body: knee, heel, toe, ankles, hips and mid-back
  3. Transform co-ordiantes and angle into 3D sound modulation
  4. guide User to stand correct in relation to:
    • Trunk / mid-back Extension
    • Hip Extension
    • Knee Exntension

Should Have
  1. Guide User to move/step correctly
  2. Make the interaction enjoyable
Could Have
  1. Encourage people to do exercise
  2. Reminds users how to practice and what type of exercise they are doing
  3. Give feedback at the end of each session
Won't Have
  1. Visual Simulation
  2. Engagement through visual Simulation
